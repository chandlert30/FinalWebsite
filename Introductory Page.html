Why Get in Shape

Getting in shape is key to living a happy and healthy life. Here at D3 Conditioning we believe that getting in shape should be fun and not a miserable experience. There are many ways to get in shape, but we have created special programs that you can do without any access to a gym.

About

D3 Conditioning is led by a current college athlete at Guilford College, Chandler Tuten. Chandler has been exercising his whole life to accomplish his dream of playing in college. Since he has accomplished that goal, he wants to help others accomplish the goal of staying in shape. He believes that the way to live a happy life, is to exercise and be in great physical condition.
