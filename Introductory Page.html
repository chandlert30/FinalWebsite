Why Get in Shape

Getting in shape is key to living a happy and healthy life. Here at D3 Conditioning we believe that getting in shape should be fun and not a miserable experience. There are many ways to get in shape, but we have created special programs that you can do without any access to a gym.












Conditioning Programs

We specialize in creating Conditioning Programs that allow you to get your body into shape within a month. We have split our programs up into two different groups. The first group "The Beginner Program" is designed for people who are out of shape right now, and are beginning, as well as, wanting to get into good shape. The second group is the "Elite Conditioning Program" this plan is for people who are already in pretty good shape but want to get into elite shape to be able to run longer distances for longer amounts of time.

Strength Programs

We also provide our customers with At Home Workout Plans because you do not always need to go to a gym to build your muscles. There are plenty of exercises you can do at home that will bring you the same, if not, more benefit than going to a gym. Plus, this will save you gas and make you more comfortable working out since you will be doing it in the comfort of your own home. We split our programs into two months, the "first month" which is more about builidng muscle, and the "second month" which is about building muscle but specializing in getting you more explosive.
