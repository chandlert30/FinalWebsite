Who is Chandler Tuten?

Learn from a Current College Basketball Player

Chandler is a college athlete that plays basketball for Guilford College. He, like many college basketball players, worked very hard to get his body to where it is to get in college basketball shape. He believes that with his experience and knowledge that he can you get into supreme shape. See below for some of Chandler's accomplishments as a high school player.







Why Learn From Chandler?

Chandler's goal has always been to play college basketball. Now that he has achieved that goal, he wants to share the workouts that helped him, to get you to accomplish your goals. Chandler did not have a lot of access to gyms, and had to create workouts on his own to get into elite physical condition. By following these programs, yow will be following in his foot steps, which will allow you to get your body to place that you will not believe.
