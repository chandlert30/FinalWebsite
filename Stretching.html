Why Stretch?

Why is Stretching Important?

Stretching is vital toward getting a good workout. Being loose will allow your body to push through exercises and do more reps. While not stretching can actually hurt and could physically injure you. D3 Conditioning has provided you with a list of stretching exercises that will get your body loose for our programs.








Stretches

  1. High Knees: Jog in place but bring your knees up to your chest.
  2. Knee Pulls: Bring your knee up to your chest with two hands and pull, do both sides. Bring your knee up, pull, then put your foot back on the ground, that’s 1.
  3. Butt Kicks:Jog in place but swing your legs back to where you are hitting your glutes with your feet gently.
  4. Standing Quad Stretch: While standing, bend your knee back and grab onto your ankle with one hand, make sure you bend your knee back as far as possible to get the full effect. Bring your leg back, grasp your ankle, pull, bring your leg back down, that’s 1.
  5. Butterfly: Sit on the ground with the soles of your feet put together. Keep your chest upward and your shoulders back, bring your feet closer to your groin for more intensity.
  6. 10 Push-Ups: Get down on all fours and place your hands slightly wider than your shoulders. Straighten your arms and legs and lower your body until you almost touch the floor, the push yourself back up
  7. Push Arm Throughs:Get down on all fours and place your hands slightly wider than your shoulders. Straighten your arms and legs and lower your body until you almost touch the floor, the push yourself back up
  8. Arm Circles: Hold both of your arms out like someone measuring your wingspan, then make circles with your hands. Start by making small circles and then continually make bigger and bigger circles
